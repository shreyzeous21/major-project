A Simple WebApp

A clean and simple interface to organize your Chats. Open a new browser tab and see your WebApp load instantly. Try it for free with my DataBase.
I made this WebApp with MERN STACK

Features

Our aim is to make it quick and easy for user to access there favorite chats. Your chats sync between your devices so you can access them on the go and they are secure for longtime we added password encryption and chat encryption on messages by react libraries so your chat are secure with us.

Single Chat Group Chat Notification On-Off user

Just Download Repo and Run

Build Things

>>messaging: Users can send and receive text messages, photos, videos, and other files.
>>Group chat: Users can create groups of up to a certain number of people and chat with each other in a group chat.

Future Build Things

>>Video chat: Users can have video conversations with each other.
>>Voice chat: Users can have voice conversations with each other.
>>Status updates: Users can share status updates with their friends and followers.
>>Sticker packs: Users can purchase or download sticker packs to use in their chats.
>>Themes: Users can change the theme of their chat app to match their personal style.

Login - Logout Features

Share

Easily share your chats and group collections with others by saving to your local devices. Create a shareable link that you can send at the click of a button. and you can share your image with your friends.
